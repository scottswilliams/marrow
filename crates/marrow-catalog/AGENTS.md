# marrow-catalog — Agent Notes

The accepted catalog semantic model: the committed snapshot (epoch, digest,
entries), its validation invariants, and structural-signature decode.
Mapped in [docs/implementation/check/](../../docs/implementation/check/README.md).

**You MUST keep this map current.** On any high-level change here — a type,
invariant, or field added, removed, renamed, or reshaped — review the catalog
identity page under `docs/implementation/check/` and update it IN PLACE in the
same change, as concisely as possible: rewrite the affected lines and delete
what went stale. It is imperative the map never accrues agentic sediment — no
appended notes, history, or duplicate lines; it is a thin map, not a changelog.
Trivial edits that change nothing at the map's altitude need no update.
